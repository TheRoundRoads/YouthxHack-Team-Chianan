Mushroom Farm

Making mushroom compost, initiated by mixing and wetting ingredients as they are stacked together.

Pasteurisation is necessary to kill any insects, nematodes, pest fungi, or other pests that may be present in the compost.

As a mushroom matures, it produces millions of microscopic spores on mushroom gills lining the underside of a mushroom cap. These spores function roughly similar to the seeds of a higher plant.

Supermarket

Most mushroom farmers harvest for 35 to 42 days, although some harvest a crop for 60 days, and harvest can go on for as long as 150 days.
