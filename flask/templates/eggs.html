Birth

The process starts with day-old chicks, carefully sourced from SFA-accredited farms to ensure they’re free from diseases. To protect them, they are vaccinated against diseases according to international standards and guidelines for poultry vaccination.

Seng Choon Farm

Besides a stress-free environment, a lot of thought goes into ensuring that the chickens are adequately fed with a proper range of nutrients. A computer system does the number-crunching for the proportion of produce used to feed the chickens, like maize and soybean. Their special diet yields eggs with excellent sources of high-quality protein, vitamins and minerals. Probiotics are used to keep chickens healthy.

The eggs are sorted, graded, scanned and cleaned with high-technology processes such as Seng Choon’s quality scanners

Supermarket

Seng Choon eggs are delivered by their own trucks to their retailers and food manufacturers. Takes less than a day from the time the egg is laid to the moment it lands on the supermarket shelf for sale
